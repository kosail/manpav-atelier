overview: "A marketplace for artisans and clients."
summary: "This project was created in response to the lack of visibility faced by artists and artisans after the closure of physical exhibition spaces. Through the design of a mobile application, it proposes a digital showcase where creators can display and sell their products, as well as promote artistic services and events. The platform connects creators directly with potential clients, based on a simple insight: what is not seen, does not sell."
additionalText: "Handmade, places local artisans and artists at the center, highlighting the value of manual creation over mass production. Each piece represents time, technique, identity, and tradition: elements that make both the object and its creator, unique. Through design, the project seeks to convey closeness, authenticity, and respect for creative processes, directly connecting artisans with people who value what is made by hand, and from the heart.

The name of this project directly refers to the raw material used to create a large portion of the country's handicrafts. It is an abundant material, deeply rooted and widely present in local culture."
