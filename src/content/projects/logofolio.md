overview: "A collection of identity marks developed across different contexts, each one shaped by the character of the brand it represents. "
summary: "The work moves between culture, product, and digital platforms, always grounded in meaning rather than style alone. The collection is not about variety for its own sake. It is about listening, translating, and constructing marks that hold meaning long after first glance."
additionalText: "Each logo begins with a question of origin. A men’s soap rooted in Mayan mythology draws from ancestral symbols and raw textures. A travel app leans into movement and discovery, shaping a visual language that feels guided yet open. A vodka brand reflects the weight and geometry of Red Square, while a performing arts company channels expression through form and rhythm.

Other identities respond to place and craft. A tourist town finds its voice through colonial windows, turning architecture into symbol. An apparel brand builds on repetition and adaptability, designed to live across materials. A platform for artisans focuses on visibility, allowing each piece to stand with clarity."
