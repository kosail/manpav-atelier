overview: "Nutrition Education Posters"
summary: "A series of educational posters designed to make nutrition understandable at a glance. Created in collaboration with a nutritionist, the work translates complex dietary concepts into clear, approachable visuals that support everyday decision making."
additionalText: "The challenge was not only to inform, but to simplify without losing accuracy. Each poster is built around a strong visual hierarchy that guides the reader from general ideas to specific details. Calories, food groups, and dietary guidelines are structured to be read quickly, yet retained.

Color and composition work as tools for orientation. They separate topics, reinforce meaning, and keep the content from feeling dense. Special attention was given to sensitive subjects such as diabetes and restricted diets, where clarity is critical.

The result is a set of pieces that feel direct and human. Information becomes visible, not overwhelming. Design steps in quietly, shaping understanding through order, rhythm, and intent."
