summary: "An event identity created for a Rover Scouts gathering, rooted in action and responsibility. Built around the idea of shaping the future through climate awareness, the work brings together symbolism, energy, and purpose into a single visual language that speaks to the spirit of the movement."
additionalText: "This project is driven by belief, not only by form. The badge and visual system were designed to carry meaning at every level. The light bulb stands for ideas and leadership. Organic shapes and leaves ground the identity in nature and sustainability. The forked stick anchors it firmly within the Rover tradition.

Color plays an active role, not decorative. It brings movement, urgency, and a sense of shared direction. Every element is composed to feel alive, to invite participation rather than observation.

The result is an identity that holds both clarity and emotion. It is recognizable, adaptable, and built to live across formats without losing strength. More than an image, it becomes a signal of intent."
